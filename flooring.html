Shiny White

White tiles have a timeless elegance that is rarely replicated by any other colour. They also exude a sense of grandeur and can work well with all kinds of colour and design schemes, making them the perfect choice for your living room.

Printed White

Print on porcelain tiles have the added benefit of being low maintenance and having a sophisticated look.They complement designs and incorporate uniqueness into your home.

Wooden Tiles

Elevate your interiors with the natural elegance of wooden tile design. These tiles integrate the warmth of natural wood without the maintenance hassles.

Checkerboard

The checkerboard floor tile pattern is as classic and elegant as it is eye-catching and versatile. Traditionally, the grid tends to be made up of black and white tiles, but depending on the style you're after, there are many other options.
